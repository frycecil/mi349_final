About

Lia Fry

Game Development Student at MSU

Hello! My name is Lia Fry, I am currently a Junior at Michigan State University focusing on 3D animation within the game development program. I have been animating for a little under a year, learning animation from the classes that Michigan State has to offer. I am primarily interested in animating for games through hand-key techniques, but am interested in learning motion capture when I have more experience under my belt. Some of my favorite games include Rainbow Six Siege, the Halo series, Life is Strange, and Telltale's The Walking Dead. I am most interested in working on games such as Life is Strange, I love story based games that have a lot of emotion and realistic movements. I want to make animations that help immerse the player into the game, the more believable the animation, the better an experience!
